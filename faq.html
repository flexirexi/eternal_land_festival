pre-register

faq.

tickets.

When does the ticket sale start?

The regular ticket sale starts on Jan 20th, 2025. Upon pre-registration, you will receive a personalized link to to vip ticket sales web page one day before the regular ticket sale. Then, you have the exclusive opportunity to get your tickets without stress.

What are the chances to get a ticket?

It highly depends from which country you are from. We sell up to 225 000 tickets. To keep it as fair as possible, each country will be given the same amount of available ticket. When clicking on the purchase button, you will draw a random waiting number. Based on that number, you have to wait in the queue until it's your turn.

Do not reload the queue page otherwise you will be put to the end of the queue.

How can I pay?

We accept VISA/MASTERCARD Credit as well as AMEX. We do not accept direct bank transfer. If you are a citizen of the United States, please note that we also accept PayPal.

What is the minimum age to buy a ticket?

Well, good news for you! You will be granted access to the ticket sale webpage when you are 18 years or older.

Can I pay the ticket prices in installments?

There is no such possibility to split the purchase price.

How can I return a ticket?

We accept ticket returns until 8 weeks before the festival starts, provided that you can prove one of the following cases:

  • sickness with evidence (such as a sick bill)
  • hospital stay
  • death
  • some other made up reasons to let you stick to the ticket.
Can I resell the tickets?

The tickets are personalized. They can't be sold to others.

journey.

There are no flight tickets left for the preferred airport. What now?

Unfortunately our flight tickets are fix. You only can choose another airport.

I lost my flight ticket. Can I still fly?

Short answer: YES. You will receive the flight ticket via email and digitally for your mobile wallet.

The worst happened, Imissed the flight! What to do?

Unfortunately, there is not much to. The only option for you would be to buy another ticket for the same day.

Is there a shuttle to the festivel?

Yes. We have chartered 5 buses who will constantly drive (roughly every 15 min);

Do I need a visa?

Please check with the office of foreign affairs in the country mentioned in your passport. Usually, visitors from the Schengen area don't need a visa. Please note that we do not take responsibility in any failure to entry Eternal Land.

the festival.

Where and when will it take place?

The festival takes place between June 27th-29th, 2025 (a weekend) on Fake Island, Bermudas ("Eternal Land").

What is the situation of the sanitary facilities?

There are a lot of lockable restrooms and toilets with floating water. For a fee, you can take a shower, too. Of course, we provide double as many toilets for women than for men. Almost no waiting time - promised.

I forgot/lost my ticket, can I still enter?

Yes. We work fully with digital tickets using QR Codes. You will receive them via email. Below the QR Code, there is a 6 digits code.

Please remember that code and everything will be fine. Besides, you can always open the email app on your phone to check.

Are there lockers for my personal belongings?

Yes. VIP tickets even include the use of one locker per person for free.

Am I allowed to bring my own drinks?

Please do not bring any drinks! We are on a relatively small island and we aim to keep rubbish under control.

However, the weather is very sunny and hot. We will provide free water at the entrance (free only at the entrance) to keep you hydrated. For that, we will give you our own cups against a little deposit. The same cups will also be used within the festival area.

What is the weather be like?

Please drink enough before you enter the festival. The weather is quite hot and sunny. Rain is not to be expected.

Is there a proper internet connection?

Of course you will have fast LTE connection. We advise you though to put your phone away, especially in the most iconic moments. Keep in mind: we are an experience festival. EDM - Love - Party - Eternaty.

Why can't I choose a hotel when buying a ticket.

Please understand that we show consideration for our local residents. Also, we want each hotel to participate in a fair manner. Most of the hotels are located with a range of 5km from the festival area.

Can I change the boot/tent?

We generally don't exchange tents or booths once you accepted them. So, please check carefully, if there are any defects. If there are defects, you can reject and require a new one.

Is there vegan/vegetarian food at the festival?

Many local food trucks will be there, offering burgers, tacos, pizzas, sandwiches, sausages etc. There will also be a huge variety of vegan and vegetarian food. Some trucks are mixed, some are vegan/vegetarian only.
