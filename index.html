ticket information.

All tickets include the flight from one of 5 airports as well as the overnight stay. First, choose your ticket category, then, choose the airport from which you want to start your journey:


  • Rent-A-Tent Ticket

    You will be given a tent package for two as soon as you enter the festival area. This package includes sleeping bags, pillows and two camping chairs. You will have access to lobkable toilets (floating water). Each eternal fan is limited to buy up to 8 tickets.

  • Booth Ticket

    For more comfort, you can rent a booth for two. It includes two beds, blankets, pillows, electricity plaugs and comfortable camping chairs. In addition, you will have a locker to secure your personal belongings. You will have access to lockable toilets (floating water). Each eternal fan is limited to buy up to 4 tickets at once.

  • Hotel Ticket

    For exclusive comfort, you will be allocated to a hotel room around the island. To each hotel, there is a shuttle every full hour. Upon request, there will be a full breakfast (also vegan/vegetarian). Please note that you can't choose the hotel. Each eternal fan is limited to buy up to 4 tickets.
